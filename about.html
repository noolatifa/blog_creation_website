Who Am I?

I’m a web developer by profession and a curious mind by nature. Balancing my time between coding, research, and personal growth, I strive to stay ahead of the curve in both technology and lifestyle. I’m currently pursuing a master’s in engineering, where I’m focusing on learning more about cloud technologies, DevOps, and expanding my technical skills. As someone who is also passionate about makeup, I’m fascinated by how beauty and technology can intersect and enhance each other. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring the latest trends in makeup or deep-diving into thought-provoking films and books. I’m on a mission to empower other women in tech and break down barriers in traditionally male-dominated spaces. Through this blog, I hope to inspire others to pursue their passions and think outside the box.

My Goals

  • To inspire others, particularly women in tech, by sharing my journey and experiences.
  • To continue expanding my knowledge in cloud computing, DevOps, and backend development.
  • To create a positive space where creativity thrives alongside technical expertise.
  • To bring my love for beauty and tech together, showing how both can be powerful tools for personal and professional growth.
  • To create a resource hub for beginners and enthusiasts in web development, cloud, and tech.
