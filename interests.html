All My Interests

IT

Technology is more than just a field for me—it’s where my creativity meets purpose. As an IT management specialist and MERN stack developer, I see every project as a chance to innovate, solve problems, and create something meaningful. Cloud computing and artificial intelligence light up my imagination, driving me to explore their endless potential. My dream of becoming an LLMOps specialist is just the beginning of a journey where learning never stops. I embrace the challenges and transformations of tech, knowing that the future isn’t just about adapting—it’s about leading. AI is not just my interest; it’s my belief, my purpose, and the space where I belong.

Research

Research, for me, is the art of turning curiosity into breakthroughs. It’s about finding the “why” and the “how” behind the questions that matter. I dream of earning a Ph.D. in AI and cloud computing to contribute to fields that are reshaping our world. My love for algorithms and problem-solving is matched only by my determination to push boundaries and unlock new possibilities. But research isn’t just about data—it’s about vision, passion, and daring to imagine a better future. Beyond academia, I channel my creativity through graphic and web design, blending technical precision with artistic flair. For me, learning is limitless, and the future is mine to shape with every new discovery.

Movies

My Journey Into Cinema

There are few things that can stir my emotions like a Christopher Nolan film. Every time I watch one of his masterpieces, it feels like I'm entering another world, and I can't help but feel a mix of awe and excitement. Nolan has this unique way of making me think, feel, and experience something entirely new with every movie. It’s like a thrilling puzzle that reveals its layers bit by bit, making me fall deeper into the narrative with each watch.

My favorite movies? *Memento (2005)*, *Fight Club*, and *Whiplash*. These are the films that not only entertain me but have completely transformed the way I view cinema. It was one person who introduced me to this universe, and I’ll always be grateful to them. Their passion for films made me realize that movies are more than just entertainment; they’re an art form that speaks to the soul.

I'm new to sharing my love for films, so feel free to check out my Letterboxd to explore more reviews and insights from me!

Music

My Musical Vibe

Let's be real: I have no specific taste when it comes to music—*I vibe on everything*! From the smoothest ballads to the deepest trap beats, music has a way of making me move, feel, and dance. I guess you could say I’m a dancing machine. Doesn’t matter if it’s hip hop, pop, or something a little more classic, if the rhythm gets me, I’m in the zone. It’s all about the beat and the vibe, baby!

Oh, and don’t get me started on Italian music... I’m obsessed! Laura Pausini, Eros Ramazzotti, they’ve got that *something special* that always hits me right in the feels. But... let’s keep this fun fact between us— I also *adore* Travis Scott. It’s kind of a weird mix, but hey, music has no rules, right? I’m all about the energy!

So, yeah, I have a bit of an eclectic taste. If you're into exploring different genres and enjoying music that makes you feel alive, you should join me on this musical journey. Who knows? Maybe we’ll end up dancing to the same beats. 💃🕺
